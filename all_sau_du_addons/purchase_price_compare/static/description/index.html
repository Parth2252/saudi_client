Purchase Price Comparison

Overview

The module, offering the capability to select purchase quotations and compare prices through a pivot view, revolutionizes the procurement landscape. By seamlessly integrating with a dynamic and interactive pivot view, users gain the power to navigate and analyze purchase quotations effortlessly. This feature not only streamlines the selection of relevant quotations but also facilitates a nuanced and comprehensive price comparison process..

No additional configuration required

Features

  • The module integrates with a pivot view, providing users with a dynamic and interactive way to analyze and compare purchase quotations.
